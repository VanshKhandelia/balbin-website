Discover Balbin

Born from a desire to elevate the ordinary, inspired by the rich heritage of South Moravia.

Renowned for its winemaking legacy and vibrant natural beauty, South-Moravia holds a deep connection to the land—a place where centuries-old traditions are celebrated, and the gifts of nature are revered. Guided by this ethos, we sought to elevate land's hidden treasure, mineral-rich water, to the same status as its cherished wines.

Still Water

Liquid Gold, from Central Europe's 20M years old underground lake

Beneath the fertile soils of South Moravia lies a pristine underground lake, protected for millions of years by dense layers of clay that once formed a grand ancient valley. Balbin water springs naturally from this safeguarded source, a true gift from the earth.
